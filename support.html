**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?** Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad and tap PolyPal. From there you can cancel or change tier. **Q: Is there a free trial?** Yes — the annual subscription includes a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges. **Q: Can I share my subscription with family?** PolyPal supports Apple's Family Sharing — your subscription will be shared with up to five family members at no extra cost.
